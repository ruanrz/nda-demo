# Qualify 'trade secret' legally

## Rule Summary

If see "trade secret", add `(as defined by applicable law)`.

## Rules

### Rule: rule_1 — Add legal definition qualifier to 'trade secret'

- **Type:** add_text
- **Trigger:** When 'trade secret' appears in the definition of Confidential Information
- **Action:** Add '(as defined by applicable law)' immediately after 'trade secret' or 'trade secrets'
- **Exact Wording:** `(as defined by applicable law)`
#### Constraints

_(none)_

#### Example

**Before:**
> The party shall not disclose any trade secret.

**After:**
> The party shall not disclose any trade secret (as defined by applicable law).

#### Rationale

If confidential information includes trade secrets, limit the scope to what is defined by applicable law to avoid unlimited extension of the definition.
