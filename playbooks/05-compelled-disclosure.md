# Compelled Disclosure & Regulatory Exception

## 1. Rule Summary

**Trigger:** Any clause discussing "Compelled Disclosure," "Legal Process," or "Disclosure Required by Law."

### A. The Required Four-Part String

Ensure the trigger is defined exactly as: **"law, rule, regulation, or legal process."** If any of these four words are missing, insert them.

### B. The Notice Qualifiers (If Notice is Required)

If the Discloser requires notice of a legal request:

- **Action:** Change "you shall" to "you shall and shall direct your Representatives to" provide notice.
- **Qualifier 1:** Add *(email being sufficient)*.
- **Qualifier 2:** Add *to the extent reasonably practicable and permitted by law*.
- **Qualifier 3 (Scope):** Recipient will only direct its Representatives to provide notice but will not provide notice on their behalf.

### C. The Cooperation Qualifiers (If Cooperation is Required)

If the Discloser requires the Recipient to cooperate:

- **Action:** Change to "you shall and shall direct your Representatives to cooperate."
- **Standard of Care:** Change to "commercially reasonable efforts" (Strike "best" or "reasonable efforts").
- **Cost:** Add "at the Disclosing Party's sole cost and expense."

### D. The "Routine Audit" Exception

**Conditional Trigger:** Only if a Notice requirement exists. Action: Add the following carveout:

> "Notwithstanding the foregoing, no notice or further action shall be required in respect of disclosure of Evaluation Material or Notes (in each case, or provision of access thereto) to regulatory authorities or self-regulatory organizations having authority over you or your Representatives in connection with a routine regulatory examination or pursuant to statutory requirements that are not specifically targeted at the Company, the Transaction or the Evaluation Material."

## 2. Polished Example for AI Processing

**Original Clause:**
> "If you are required by law to disclose the Evaluation Material, you shall provide notice to the Company and use best efforts to cooperate with the Company..."

**Polished Version (AI-Optimized):**
> "If you or your Representatives are required by law, rule, regulation, or legal process to disclose the Evaluation Material, you shall and shall direct your Representatives to (to the extent reasonably practicable and permitted by law) provide the Company with prompt written notice (email being sufficient); provided that you shall not be required to provide notice on behalf of your Representatives. You shall and shall direct your Representatives to cooperate with the Company, using commercially reasonable efforts and at the Disclosing Party's sole cost and expense, to obtain a protective order. Notwithstanding the foregoing, no notice or further action shall be required in respect of disclosure... [Insert Routine Audit Exception]."

## 3. Strategic "Why" for AI Reasoning

"This modification replaces strict personal liability with a Covenant to Direct. It ensures the Recipient isn't in breach if a Representative (like an outside bank) handles its own regulatory notice, while shifting the financial burden of protecting the Discloser's data back to the Discloser."
